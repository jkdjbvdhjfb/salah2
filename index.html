Solo Leveling

Welcome to the world of Solo Leveling. Dive into the adventures of Sung Jin-Woo as he becomes the strongest hunter in a world filled with monsters and magic.

About Solo Leveling

Solo Leveling is a South Korean web novel written by Chu-Gong. It was serialized in Kakao's digital comic and fiction platform KakaoPage since July 25, 2016, and it was later published by D&C Media under their Papyrus label since November 4, 2016.

Main Characters

Sung Jin-Woo

The protagonist of Solo Leveling. Initially the weakest hunter, he later becomes the strongest through mysterious powers.

Cha Hae-In

An S-Rank Hunter and the vice-guild master of the Hunters Guild. She becomes an important ally and friend to Jin-Woo.
